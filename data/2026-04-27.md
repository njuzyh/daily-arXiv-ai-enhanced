Abstract: Real-time communications (RTC) is a core technology for emerging applications in 6G, such as cloud gaming, teleoperation, and extended reality (XR), which require consistently low latency and high bitrates. Existing RTC solutions fundamentally struggle to maintain low latency while supporting high bitrates due to their reliance on trial-and-error-based mechanisms.
