No infinite-family theorem in this manuscript is proved by finite witnesses or by computational enumeration. The theorem scope is stated for the parameter ranges where an algebraic complement-incidence certificate is written down. Tables and CSV data are used only to verify and reproduce the formulas, never as proof of an infinite-family theorem.
